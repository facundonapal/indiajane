Situated in the Royal Borough of Kensington and Chelsea, the light and airy  India Jane store at 130 Kensington High Street, is thoughtfully arranged over two floors and houses comprehensive selections of our collections. Wonderfully proportioned interior spaces create an enveloping and fragrant ambience, that is bathed in the gentle luminosity of twinkling chandeliers, adding sparkle and glamour within the store. Both restful to the eye and calming to the senses, it offers an inviting sanctuary from hectic urban life.

Housed in a glorious Georgian building with its distinctive curved glass windows and a magnificent original staircase, 2 Ormond Place in Cheltenham is the newest addition of the India Jane collection of stores.

Its interior embraces an aesthetic that is welcoming, relaxed and refined, thoughtfully composed and visually pleasing. Comfortable sofas and armchairs within discreet alcoves create intimate seating areas and elegantly set dining tables dressed with softly gleaming silver and glass, providing inspiration for entertaining and gracious living. Both floors offer unusual and interesting collections of furniture, mirrors, prints, original pieces and both decorative and functional accessories, offer a wealth of inspiration for the home. Two dedicated lighting departments offer plenty of choice across our wide and varied range with a selection of lamp shades close by. India Janes friendly staff are welcoming, helpful and pleased to offer assistance and advice.
